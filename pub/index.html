PermForm.js

Your one stop shop for building seamless forms.

Simplicity

With just one line of code, you can produce a beautiful, customizable, form which you can customize with ease to produce complex forms to your liking.

Autosave

A simple true/false toggle to make your form automatically save the state of each form element, and reloading when the user visits your site the next time. Say goodbye to users losing their hard work to input data!

Grid

Use the powerful grid system to nest your objects, and have PermForm automatically place them in a grid according to your nested objects. Modify where each element is located on the grid with ease!

Drag-and-drop

A state-of-the-art drag-and-drop system for all your rapid development needs, you can play around with the design and export the JSON schema of the form when you're done!

Validation

With one small setting, PermForm automatically validates user input!
